Classic Tiramisu Recipe

Tiramisu is an Italian dessert made of biscuits dipped in coffee, layered with a whipped mixture of eggs, sugar and mascarpone and flavored with cocoa. The recipe has been adapted into many varieties of cakes and other desserts.

Ingredients:

  • Biscuits (300 g)
  • Sweetened Condensed Milk (370g)
  • Cream (170g)
  • Cream Cheese (200 g)
  • Coffee (2 tablespoons)
  • Water (200 ml)
  • Cocoa Powder (40 g)

Steps:

  • In a large bowl combine the cream cheese, cream and sweetened condensed milk. Whisk well until soft and smooth.
  • Add the coffee to the water and stir until dissolve. Start dipping the biscuits in the coffee and layer them at the bottom of the surving cup.
  • Top the biscuitslayer with a generous layer of the cream mix.
  • Repeat the layering once again with each layer of biscuits topped with another layer of the cream mix.
  • Using a small sieve sprinkle a dash of cocoa powder on top.
  • Chill for 4 hours and serve cold.
